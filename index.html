Well, these are the normal problems and what about longings to have a closet full of trendy clothes with a distinct 'sexy' element for special outings or cocktails; or that Anarkali you saw in that TV show on your favourite star, and is out of your reach owing to inability of your tailor to stitch it? Forget about those vain attempts made by you to make your local tailor understand the latest neck lines or styles as now, you have the expert designers (catering to a global clientele of over 180 countries) available at one phone call to design and stitch a unique, trendy, CUSTOMIZED ethnic dress for you.

Getting Things Done:
SIGN UP for the Ethnostitch Online Tailoring cum Customization service (Its FREE) Choose the apparel you wish to get custom stitched out of Blouse, Salwar Kameez, Bottom and Anarkali. You will be able to choose the styles such as Sexy, Classic or Trendy etc. A simple menu will help you to make customization for the dress length, neck lines, sleeves, hemline, openings, and other add-ons etc. Finally, you are to pack ONE SAMPLE and the FABRIC and place a call to book a free pickup for the Ethnostitch Online Tailoring and Customization. The finished product will be delivered to your doorstep within 7 to 10 days and that too at nominal charges.
